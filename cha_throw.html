Thow

Genre: Challenge

Level: ★★

Introduction:
After the game starts, the following instructions will appear at the top of the screen:
1. Please show your full body and hold the position for five seconds.
2. Please turn to your left side, place your hand behind your head, and after holding for 3 seconds.
3. Start throwing.
Tips: The faster you swing your hand and the more distinct the separation of your feet, the higher your score will be.
