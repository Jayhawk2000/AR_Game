Jump

Genre: Challenge

Level: ★★★

Introduction:

After the game starts, the following instructions will appear at the top of the screen:

1. Please show your full body and hold the position for five seconds.

2. Please slightly bend your knees and squat holding the position for three seconds.

3. Please push off the ground and jump.

Tips:

The higher you jump and the longer you keep your feet apart, the higher your score will be.
